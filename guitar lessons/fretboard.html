The fretboard is how you change the sound of the guitar. They work in keys, e.g. on the top sting the open note is E if you press the first fret of the top string it becomes and F, so on.

Notes Across the Strings

Memorizing the notes on the fretboard can seem tricky, but learning a few patterns can make it simple. Start by memorizing the natural notes (A, B, C, D, E, F, G) on the low E and A strings. Then its good to learn the string names. from low to high (E, A, D, G, B, E). These are the keys for all the strings in open.
