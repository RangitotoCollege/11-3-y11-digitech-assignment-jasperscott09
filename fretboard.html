Understanding the Guitar Fretboard

The fretboard is the technical side of your guitar — every note, chord, and scale you’ll ever play is here. Understanding it will help you use the instrument with ease.

📍 The Layout of the Fretboard

The fretboard is how you change the sound of the guitar. They work in keys, e.g. on the top sting the open note is E if you press the first fret of the top string it becomes and F, so on.
